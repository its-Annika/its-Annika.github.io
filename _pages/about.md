I'm a first year PhD student in [Linguistics](https://linguistics.umd.edu/) at the University of Maryland, advised by Profs. [Naomi Feldman](https://users.umiacs.umd.edu/~nhf/) and [Bill Idsard](https://wjidsardi.net/). My research is supported by the NSF GRFP. 

My research centers on one question: how do monolingual listeners perceive non-native speech sounds? I approach this question from a computational perspective by using machine learning and computational modeling to understand both human and machine speech perception.
